**Usage**: Copy this prompt, fill in [PLACEHOLDERS], give to Claude Code for any tier's LinkedIn post.

## THE PROMPT

```
Create a LinkedIn post for Digital FTE — [TIER_NAME] Tier.

**Context:**
- Author: Shahzain Ali — Agentic AI Developer | Specializing in Automation & Chatbots | Building Intelligent Agents Globally
- Brand (future): Agentive Solution
- Product: Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) — locally hosted AI employee
- This is [TIER_NUMBER] of 4 tiers: Bronze (local file processing) → Silver (email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn) → Gold (ERP, social media, cross-platform) → Platinum (multi-agent, voice)

**This tier adds:**
- [NEW_CAPABILITY_1]
- [NEW_CAPABILITY_2]
- [NEW_CAPABILITY_3]

**Tech stack for this tier:**
- [TECH_1]
- [TECH_2]
- [TECH_3]

**Demo/proof I'll attach:**
- [SCREENSHOT_OR_VIDEO_DESCRIPTION_1]
- [SCREENSHOT_OR_VIDEO_DESCRIPTION_2]

**Post requirements:**
1. Tone: Thought leadership + Educational
2. Length: Medium (detailed but scannable)
3. Target audience: Business owners, CTOs, entrepreneurs, developers, general LinkedIn — anyone who might hire me or collaborate globally (local + international remote)
4. Start with a hook — industry insight or question
5. Briefly recap what Digital FTE is (1-2 lines, link to Bronze post if needed)
6. Focus on what THIS tier adds and why it matters for businesses
7. Include stat/number that shows business value (cost saving, time saving, efficiency)
8. Show the tech stack as a clean table
9. Mention SpecKit Plus (spec-driven development) briefly
10. End with CTA — connect, hire, collaborate
11. Add relevant hashtags (25-30)
12. Include screenshot order guide
13. Do NOT mention "hackathon" — position as a professional product tier
14. Do NOT use emojis excessively — max 3-4 in entire post
15. Language: English, professional but accessible to non-tech readers
```
